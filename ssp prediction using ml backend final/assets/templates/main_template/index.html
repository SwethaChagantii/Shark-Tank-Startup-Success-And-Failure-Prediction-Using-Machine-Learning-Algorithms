About Shark Tank Show

Shark Tank is a popular reality TV show where entrepreneurs pitch their business ideas to a panel of potential investors, referred to as "sharks." The sharks are successful entrepreneurs and investors who consider investing their own money in exchange for equity in the entrepreneurs' businesses.

Contestants negotiate deals with the sharks, aiming to secure investment to help grow their businesses. The show provides a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas and potentially receive funding, mentorship, and exposure for their businesses.
